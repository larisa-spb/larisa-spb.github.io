About company

About our company and our activities

Life is a journey!

For 14 years, the travel company "PETERTUR" has been working in the tourism market of exotic countries, and every year opens up new countries for Russian travelers. Prepares unique offers with a deep understanding of the subject. A great knowledge of the business allows us with an equally high level of quality to form a tourist product of any level of complexity and price policy, as well as exclusive tour packages. But also with great pleasure we select programs for tourists who want to open new corners of the world or learn their favorite countries in new conditions.

All this was made possible thanks to a unique team of managers, operators and the management of the company "Petertert", which annually send more than ten thousand people to rest. The average work experience in the areas ranges from 3 to 8 years.
